Stripe

You can get going with our Stripe sims within minutes of signing up, no need to wait for API Keys and access codes. Follow our example apps and code to start off your integration using Ruby, Java, PHP, etc.

Our Stripe sims include:

  • Charges - for one-off transactions
  • Tokens - tokenising card data, reducing your PCI compliance level and increased security
  • Subscriptions - managing monthly / annual fee type transactions
  • Customers - managing your customer data for charges and subscriptions
  • Coupons - offer discounts and vouchers for your customers
  • Refunds - issue a refund to your customers based on a charge / subscription

With the sims above you can get your code off the ground, and prepare for hard-to-cover scenarios like rate limit errors and card-currency mismatch!
